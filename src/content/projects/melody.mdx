I co-founded **Melody** (Flow With The Seasons) as the sole mobile developer — taking a women's health idea from blank canvas to App Store and Play Store alongside a non-technical product co-founder.

Melody frames the cycle in seasons — spring, summer, autumn, winter — so patterns feel intuitive rather than clinical. The home screen centres on a colour-coded cycle ring that shows where you are today, which phase you're in, and what's coming next.

## Under the hood

Behind the polished UI sits a **Melos monorepo** with shared packages, a Widgetbook design-system workspace, and a separate **privacy-first offline tracker** with Hive encryption. Firebase powers auth, Firestore, and 12+ Cloud Functions; RevenueCat handles freemium subscriptions.

With no other mobile engineer on the team, quality had to be built in: **250+ test files** spanning unit, widget, golden, BDD, integration (against real Gemini), and Patrol E2E. Fastlane and GitHub Actions ship to both stores; Shorebird patches production without waiting on store review.
