Discover the bird life around Redland Green in Bristol with this beautiful app created with stunning photos by local wildlife photographer **Giorgio Pede**. Help to monitor the birdlife by observing the bird boxes and entering your observations into the app for others to see.

Nominated for a Your Park Bristol & Bath award.
