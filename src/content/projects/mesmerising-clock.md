Mesmerising Clock started as an entry in the Flutter Clock Challenge and has grown into a polished indie app with custom colour palettes, night mode, adjustable hand thickness, and a Wear OS companion.

Built with Flutter and minimal dependencies — a focused example of thoughtful UI, persistence, and multi-form-factor delivery without backend complexity.
